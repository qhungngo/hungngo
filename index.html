Weighted Statistical Binary Patterns Framework

We present a novel framework for facial feature representation based upon Local Binary Pattern, called Weighted Statistical Binary Pattern. The descriptors utilize the straight-line topology by direction for Local Binary Pattern (LBP) and have local difference sign-magnitude distributions on two-moment images composed for spatial statistic support. This approach is an extension of the Completed LBP, where the useful information on moment variations for a weighting approach is utilized. Evaluation using four standard face datasets suggest that the present framework outperforms the state-of-the-art methods and achieves 98.51% for ORL, 98.72% for YALE, 98.83% for Caltech, and 99.52% for AR dataset in terms of accuracy, respectively. Experimental results suggest that our descriptors are robust against noise, illumination conditions, diverse facial expressions, and head pose changes.
